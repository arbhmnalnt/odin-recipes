Peach Salsa

description

Looking to use up your bounty of summertime peaches? Look no further than these deliciously sweet and savory peach salsa recipes that are the ultimate snack to cool you off on a warm summer's day. From spicy peach salsa packed with peppers to sweet peach salsa paired with more seasonal fruits, these fresh salsas are extra tasty served with tortilla chips and graham crackers — and even on tacos or your favorite grilled meats. Ingredients

  • 4 cups fresh peaches - peeled, pitted and chopped
  • ½ cup chopped onion
  • ½ cup chopped red bell pepper

Steps

  1. Sterilize jars and lids.
  2. In a large saucepan, combine peaches, onion, pepper, cilantro, garlic, cumin, vinegar, and lime zest. Stir together pectin and 1/4 cup sugar. Bring to a boil, and stir in remaining 3 1/2 cups sugar. Boil for 1 minute, stirring constantly. Remove from heat, and stir for 5 minutes.
  3. Pour peach salsa into prepared jars, leaving 1/4 inch air space. Seal, and process in a boiling water bath for 15 minutes.
